Memory Management - Entry Three

Another way to create a strong retain cycle is while using closures.
Here we are going to add a closure to our bank account that will allow you to increment your account balance.
When customer and account are outside of scope, the customer is deinitialized, but account is not.
This is because account has a strong reference to account from inside our added closure.
